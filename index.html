ABOUT US

Who we Are

Up-strike Football Club was Founded in 2022,Up-strike Football Club is a youth focused football academy based in Abuja, Nigeria. We are dedicated to identifying, developing, and nurturing young football talents through structured training, discipline, and professional guidance.

Our Mission

To develop young football talents through professional coaching and structured training
To promote discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship
To provide a safe and supportive football environment
To prepare players for competitive football and future opportunities

TRAINING
PHILOSOPHY

At Up-strike Football Club, our training philosophy is built on development, discipline, and excellence. We focus on nurturing players holistically,technically, tactically, physically, and mentally.While instilling strong values on and off the pitch.

Our training programs emphasize technical mastery, intelligent decision making, and game understanding, ensuring players are confident and effective in real match situations. We believe in structured, age-appropriate training sessions that promote creativity, teamwork, and adaptability.

Physical conditioning and injury prevention are key components of our philosophy, supported by professional fitness programs tailored to each players development stage. Equally important is mental strength, where players are taught resilience, focus, and leadership.
